Civic activism

Human rights education

As part of NGO 'Youth Information Service of Kazakhstan', I contribute to the development of civic society and human rights education promotion in Kazakhstan. The most recent project that I have worked on as a media coordinator is "1SRIGHTS" – fairy tales for children about laws and basic human rights (October 2022–May 2023).

Female participation in politics

I graduated from the project “Purple Yurt” that is aimed at increasing female political participation. During the course I studied political organization of Kazakhstan, feminist theory and history (July 2022–December 2022).

Environmental activism

I am president of “NU Green Society” student club for promotion of sustainable development ideas among university students and beyond (April 2023–continuing), member of “SOS.Taldykol” civic movement for preservation of natural lakes in Astana (August 2022–continuing) and participant of “Local Conference of Youth” by United Nations Development Program for youth inclusion in topics of environmental protection (November 2022).

Democratic values advocacy

I am an independent obzerver at state elections and I participate in peaceful protests (October 2022–continuing).
