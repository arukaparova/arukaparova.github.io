Do Direct Elections Have Impact on Female Representation in Government Leadership positions?

Since 2021 'akims' in rural districts and villages in Kazakhstan are directly elected. I interviewed female 'akims' who came to the incumbent office after this insitutional change to examine relationship between elections and female politicians' representation.

How can Kazakhstan reduce homelessness level?

Widely margninalized homeless people rarely exits their social group for long once they enter. Considering the growing number of homeless people in Kazakhstan, I attempted to identify the most efficient ways of solving the problem.
